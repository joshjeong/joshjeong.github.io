Week 3: Pairing

Before Dev Bootcamp, a majority of my self studies was spent scratching my head or yelling at my screen as if my programming error was my computer’s fault. Coding obstacles were hard to overcome; I would have a difficult time debugging or fully grasping a concept. Pairing has been a refreshing take. It’s been a great experience talking issues out and gaining another perspective- two minds are truly better than one.

Feedback has been insightful. It’s not like the instructor’s have been demoralizing me, but instead, have been providing constructive criticism. They have pointed out that I need to be more vocal when I get stuck with an issue which is completely true. I need to be able to think aloud so that my partner and I may be on the same page. I want to implement this change as soon as possible but it is a skill that continuously needs to be improved.

Writing feedback has been difficult because the people I have worked with have been more than helpful. I feel it is hard to rate others especially when we are all in a learning process. Overall, I love the pairing experience and look forward to future sessions.
